## About the Project

At Viva La Vida, your home is more than just a space, it’s an experience, a way of life. Step into a world where nature, comfort, and modern luxury exist in perfect harmony. Nestled in Kottawa, Viva La Vida invites you to embrace the fullness of life, surrounded by elegance and nature, where you can long live life with nature.

Nestled in the heart of Kottawa, just minutes from the Southern Expressway Interchange, our exclusive community of 78 Signature Villas offers families a rare chance to embrace life fully.

Location Highlights

* 5 minutes to Dharmapala College
* 5 minutes to Pannipitiya Nursing Home
* 10 minutes to the Highway Interchange
* 10 minutes to Maharagama Town
* Surrounded by supermarkets, leading hospitals, medical centers, shopping complexes, restaurants, and cafes

Amenities

* Swimming pool
* Gymnasium
* Electric car charging point
* Jogging track
* 24-hour security
* Floral garden
* Yoga & meditation area
* Landscaped areas
* Camping areas

Our signature open kitchen and pantry redefine modern living, honoring the homemaker and making cooking a joyful experience amidst grace, connection, and nature’s gentle embrace. With spacious layouts, natural ventilation, and lush greenery, families flourish while children grow among flowers, butterflies, birds, and paddy fields, nurturing curiosity and love for nature.

At Viva La Vida, Kottawa, we don’t just build homes, we cultivate a community where both body and spirit thrive, a place to truly live in harmony with nature. Slow down, breathe deep, and enjoy life the way it is meant to be.
